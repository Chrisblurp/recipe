Classic Spaghetti Bolognese

A hearty Italian pasta dish with rich, slow-cooked meat sauce. Perfect for family dinners and guaranteed to impress guests.

Recipe Information

Preparation time: 15 minutes

Cooking time: 1 hour

Servings: 4 people

Difficulty: Intermediate

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 500g ground beef
  • 400g canned chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Freshly grated Parmesan cheese (for serving)

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Add the onion and cook for 5 minutes until soft, then add the garlic and cook for another minute.
  3. Increase heat to medium-high, add the ground beef, and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in the tomato paste, chopped tomatoes, oregano, and basil.
  5. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 45 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Meanwhile, cook the spaghetti in salted boiling water according to the package instructions.
  7. Drain spaghetti, top with the sauce, and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.

Tips

For extra flavor, add a splash of red wine to the sauce while simmering. You can also use a mix of beef and pork for a richer taste.
